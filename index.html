Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I rent a Pocketalk translation device in Thailand?+
Crestec (Thailand) provides Pocketalk device rental on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis. Contact us at 02-714-3050 or info@crestec.co.th for a quote.
What is the difference between Pocketalk Conference and Live Translation?+
Pocketalk Conference (Sentio) is designed for large events with hundreds of attendees simultaneously. Live Translation suits smaller groups — participants join via QR code on any browser, no app needed.
Does Crestec sell Pocketalk devices?+
We no longer sell devices. For purchases, contact SiS Distribution (sisthai.com). Crestec provides rental, Live Translation, and Conference services only.
Can I rent Pocketalk devices for factory use in Thailand?+
Yes. Crestec (Thailand) provides Pocketalk rental for Japanese-owned factories and industrial environments. Supports Japanese, Thai, English, and 80+ languages. Contact 02-714-3050 or info@crestec.co.th.
Do Pocketalk rental devices need Wi-Fi or a SIM card?+
Rental devices require a Wi-Fi connection — no SIM card or eSIM is included. Please ensure Wi-Fi is available at your location before renting.
